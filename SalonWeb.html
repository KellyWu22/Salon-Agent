Hair Haven Salon

Salon Agent Demo

This demo lets visitors ask about salon services, pricing, stylist availability, and appointment guidance through the deployed Vertex AI conversational agent.

  • Ask about services such as classic cuts, pixie cuts, balayage, and highlights.
  • Check stylist availability for Clifton, Hazel, and Rowan.
  • Get pricing guidance and salon contact details.
  • Receive booking guidance, with long-duration services redirected to phone support.

If the chat does not load for external users, verify your deployment permissions, token broker settings, and any allowed-origin restrictions in Google Cloud.
